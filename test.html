Instagram Video Enhancer - Extension Test

Installation Instructions:

  1. Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions/
  2. Enable "Developer mode" (toggle in top-right)
  3. Click "Load unpacked"
  4. Select the insta_extension folder
  5. Go to Instagram.com to test the extension

How it works:

  • Hover over Instagram videos to see control bar
  • Use timeline slider to seek through video
  • Click rotate button (↻) to rotate video 90°
  • Use scale slider to zoom in/out (50%-300%)
  • Click fullscreen button (⛶) for fullscreen mode

Keyboard Shortcuts:

  • Ctrl+Shift+R - Rotate clockwise
  • Ctrl+Shift+L - Rotate counter-clockwise
  • Ctrl+Shift+0 - Reset all transformations
  • Ctrl+Shift+F - Toggle fullscreen
